Frequently Asked Questions

What are the flight rules and regulations for flying drones?

Flying drones is subject to different flight rules and regulations depending on the country. In most cases, drone pilots need to be registered, adhere to specific altitudes, and follow no-fly zones. Make sure to educate yourself about the applicable rules before flying and operate responsibly.

What camera resolution do your camera drones offer?

Our camera drones offer a wide range of camera resolutions, ranging from Full HD to 4K. The resolution depends on the model variant. We recommend visiting the respective product pages to get detailed information about camera resolution and other features.

What is the flight time of your drones?

The flight time varies depending on the model and usage. Our drones typically offer flight times of 15 to 30 minutes per battery charge. However, please note that factors like wind speed and flight maneuvers can affect the actual flight time.

Can I get replacement parts for my drone?

Yes, we provide a wide range of replacement parts and accessories for our drones. From spare batteries to propellers and camera modules, we have everything you need to keep your drone in top condition. Visit our accessories page to learn more.

How can I maintain and care for my drone?

Maintaining your drone is crucial to ensure optimal performance. Be sure to regularly check the propellers for damage, store the batteries properly, and keep the firmware up to date. For specific care instructions, we recommend consulting our user manual.
